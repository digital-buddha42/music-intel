## What To Listen For

1. **The horns in the Phish songs.** TAB arrangements of Phish songs like Gin or Limb By Limb hit differently with the horn section landing parts that Trey normally carries alone. Red Rocks acoustics make this especially good.

2. **Whether they extend anything.** TAB jams are more contained than Phish, but Red Rocks and a hot Colorado crowd sometimes push Trey into stretching. If Sand or Walls of the Cave opens up past the usual structure, something special is happening.

3. **Trey's guitar tone.** Coming off the Sphere run (which had its own technical constraints), a straight outdoor show where he just has his pedalboard and the night air tends to bring out his clearest, most melodic playing.
