3. **New drummer Marlon B. Lewis in a seated room** — The Paramount is a legit theater. Tighter acoustics relative to the festival/bowl circuit means you'll really hear how Marlon is reshaping the groove. He's been pushing harder into the trance-fusion pocket than Allen; watch how the band responds to it on slower builds like Scars or Voices Insane.
